ABOUT CLOVE

Our Product

Growing your own food at home should not be a difficult or time consuming process that doesn't always end with a substantial harvest. More than ever, people want fresh, locally sourced ingredients for the food they eat and serve to their families and friends. Our goal is to design a product that ensures your success and provides you an opportunity to experience the immense sense of pride that comes along with sustaining a healthy and high yield garden. Through our system, we will make even the most inept gardener a master of growing nearly whatever they want, whenever they want. In doing so, we hope to give you more time and energy to focus on the things that really matter in life like friends, family, and crafting the next tasty meal you plan to share with them.
